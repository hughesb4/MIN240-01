Climber Companion

Bringing together beginner climbers with seasoned professionals

Climber Companion is an app that brings together individuals who love climbing. The is designed to connect budding climbers with seasoned professionals who know how to safely and effectivley climb.

With the help of the app, you can find a way to connect in multiple ways...

Classes that will put you with an instructor in a private or group setting so when you climb, you can feel confident and prepared

Volunteering, maybe you aren't sure if you are ready to climb outdoors climbing want to get in the area and around others who share your interest in climbing; volunteer to clean up and perform trail maintenance in and around local crags.

Belay Buddy Finder, with this feature, you have climbed outdoors before, done top rope or sport climbing in your local gym and are in need of a belay buddy to support you in your climbing.
